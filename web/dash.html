SCMxPerlite is a comprehensive supply chain management platform designed specifically for efficient perlite transportation. It harnesses device data streams and provides seamless shipment creation capabilities, offering the following features:


Device Data Stream:

Utilizes device data streams including device ID, battery level, temperature, route information. Enables real-time tracking for transparent monitoring of shipment location and condition. Ensures uninterrupted device functionality and timely maintenance through battery level monitoring. Maintains optimal conditions for perlite transportation with temperature data tracking. Improves efficiency and selects the most suitable transportation path through route optimization.


Creating Shipments:

Facilitates shipment creation with essential details like shipment number, container number, goods type, route, PO number, batch ID, delivery number, and more. Streamlines the process, minimizing errors, reducing manual effort, and improving efficiency. Provides comprehensive shipment documentation, ensuring regulatory compliance. Enables real-time tracking and status updates for users to stay informed. Enhances collaboration and communication among stakeholders, facilitating seamless information sharing and issue resolution.


SCMxPerlite empowers businesses engaged in perlite transportation by optimizing supply chain operations, improving visibility, and streamlining shipment management processes, ultimately resulting in enhanced customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
