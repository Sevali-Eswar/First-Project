SCMxPerlite is a comprehensive supply chain management platform designed specifically for efficient perlite transportation. It harnesses device data streams and provides seamless shipment creation capabilities, offering the following features:


Device Data Stream:

Utilizes device data streams including device ID, battery level, first sensor temperature, route from, and route to. Real-time tracking allows users to monitor shipment location and condition, ensuring transparency and proactive decision-making. Battery level monitoring ensures uninterrupted device functionality and timely maintenance. Temperature data tracking helps maintain optimal conditions for perlite transportation. Route optimization based on route details improves efficiency and helps in selecting the most suitable transportation path.


Creating Shipments:

Facilitates the creation of shipments by incorporating essential details such as shipment number, container number, device, goods type, route details, PO number, batch ID, delivery number, serial number of goods, shipment description, expected delivery date, and NOC number. Streamlines the process to minimize errors, reduce manual effort, and improve overall efficiency. Provides comprehensive shipment documentation, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. Enables real-time tracking and status updates, allowing users to stay informed about the progress of their shipments. Enhances collaboration and communication among stakeholders involved in the shipment process, facilitating seamless information sharing and issue resolution.


SCMxPerlite empowers businesses engaged in perlite transportation by optimizing supply chain operations, improving visibility, and streamlining shipment management processes, ultimately resulting in enhanced customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
